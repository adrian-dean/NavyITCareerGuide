Your Navy IT Career Roadmap

Follow each step of progression from A-School to specialized training, NECs, leadership, and beyond.

Step 1: Accession & A-School

Complete boot camp, then head to Pensacola for IT A-School. Earn foundational skills in networking, security, and systems support.

Step 2: Initial C-School (CANES / ADNS / IATO)

Begin your first C-School pipeline and earn your first NEC. Start your fleet tour on a platform like a ship or sub.

Step 3: Specialization

Get selected for cyber billets (743A/B), JCAC, or pursue network admin certifications (U03A). Apply for billet-based advancement tracks.

Step 4: Leadership & Instruction

Promote to LPO or IT1. Go 805A and earn MTS. Become a platform lead, section supervisor, or classroom instructor.

Step 5: Strategic Roles & Degree Completion

Serve at Cyber Command, ECRC, or recruit training. Finish your college degree using JST and certifications for credit.
