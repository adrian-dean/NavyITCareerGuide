Welcome to the Navy IT Career Guide

This site is built to help current and prospective Navy Information Systems Technicians (ITs) understand every aspect of their career path, from A-school to advanced cyber warfare roles.

Explore:

  • Programs: Learn about rating-specific programs like JCAC, CANES, CWT, and CMF.
  • Training: Understand NECs, C-schools, and billet-based advancement.
  • Education: Maximize JST credit using Coursera, edX, and Navy eLearning.
  • Career Roadmap: Follow a suggested progression from E1 to E9 with cyber or leadership specialization.
  • Resources: Access NAVADMINs, detailer contacts, and training portals.
