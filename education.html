Turn Training into Degrees

Use JST credits, certification pathways, and online platforms to transform your Navy experience into formal education.

Joint Services Transcript (JST)

Automatically builds your college transcript from military schools. Submit to accredited universities to earn credit toward degrees.

Visit JST Portal

Coursera & Udemy

Use Navy-sponsored access or self-pay to complete courses in cybersecurity, networking, cloud, and more. Some offer ACE credit recommendations.

Visit Coursera

Western Governors University (WGU)

Fully online, competency-based degrees. Accepts JST, certs like CompTIA and Cisco, and offers scholarships for veterans.

Explore WGU

Certification Paths

Navy COOL helps fund certifications like CompTIA Security+, Cisco CCNA, AWS, and more. Boost your resume and get civilian-recognized skills.

Go to Navy COOL
