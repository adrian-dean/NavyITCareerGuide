IT-Specific Programs

JCAC (Joint Cyber Analysis Course)

A 6-month intensive school for cyber warfare preparation. ITs may attend and either remain in-rate or convert to Cyber Warfare Technician (CWT).

CANES (Consolidated Afloat Networks and Enterprise Services)

A shipboard system training pipeline. Completion earns the H09A NEC and qualifies ITs for network admin afloat billets.

Cyber Mission Force (CMF)

Elite cyber operations teams under U.S. Cyber Command. ITs in cyber NECs may qualify for these highly selective roles and billet-based advancements.

CWT Rating Transition

After JCAC, ITs may choose to convert into the CWT rating or retain IT designation with cyber-focused NECs like 743A, 743B, or 741A.

Instructor Track (805A / 9502)

ITs can become certified instructors through the 805A school and Master Training Specialist (MTS) 9502 designation for leadership/shore roles.
