Special Warfare & Elite Cyber Programs

These exclusive programs offer high-intensity training, joint operations, and access to the most elite assignments in the Information Warfare domain.

Joint Communications Unit (JCU)

Provides tactical communications support to special operations forces worldwide. Operates in joint environments under time-sensitive conditions.

Joint Cyber Operations Group (JCOG)

Performs joint cyber operations in partnership with U.S. Cyber Command and allied cyber defense agencies.

JSOC Intelligence Brigade (JIB)

Supports Joint Special Operations Command with intelligence preparation, exploitation, and targeting for elite missions.

Navy Expeditionary Intelligence Command (NEIC)

Conducts expeditionary ISR operations to support Naval and Joint Force missions, including tactical exploitation and human intelligence.

Naval Special Warfare Development Group (NSWDG)

Supports SEAL Team 6 with communications, intel, and mission planning for the most classified and sensitive operations.
