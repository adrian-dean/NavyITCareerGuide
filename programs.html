Information Warfare Special Programs

Explore unique assignments available to Navy ITs in the Information Warfare community.

Defense Attaché System (DAS)

Support global intelligence operations by assisting diplomatic missions in U.S. embassies abroad.

Embassy Duty / Attaché Support

Work directly in U.S. embassies to provide classified communications and support intelligence missions.

Fleet Cyber Command (FCC)

Participate in cyber operations, network defense, and global cyber readiness under U.S. TENTH Fleet.

Joint Communications Unit (JCU)

Support special operations with rapid-deploy communication solutions for tactical missions worldwide.

National Reconnaissance Office (NRO)

Engage in top-secret space and satellite operations in support of national security missions.

National Security Agency (NSA)

Work in cryptologic and cyber defense operations supporting U.S. and allied missions worldwide.

Office of Naval Intelligence (ONI)

Serve with ONI in gathering, analyzing, and reporting operational intelligence and fleet security data.

Presidential Support Duty (PSD)

Support the White House Communications Agency or Marine One with secure, high-profile communications systems.

Recruit Division Commander (RDC)

Lead and mentor new recruits at RTC Great Lakes. A challenging billet that shapes the Navy’s future Sailors.

US Cyber Command (USCYBERCOM)

Engage in offensive and defensive cyber operations under joint military command and strategic cyber teams.

White House Communications Agency (WHCA)

Provide direct communications support to the President, Vice President, and Secret Service operations.
